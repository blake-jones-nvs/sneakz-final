What is Sneakz?

Sneakz is a peer-to-peer rental platform with the mission of enabling Sneakerheads throughout the U.S. to wear the freshest kicks, for affordable prices.

How does it work?

If you're a sneakerhead with shoes to spare, sign up to rent out your shoes to other heads. If you're a sneakerhead looking to try new looks, or wanting to look your best for an event, sign up to rent your favorite pair of sneakers.
